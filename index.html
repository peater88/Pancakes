ü•û The Best Pancake Recipe

Fluffy, easy, and totally delicious. This is your go-to recipe for pancakes everyone will love!

Ingredients:

  • 1000 ml milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 500 g flour
  • 1 tbsp rum (optional)
  • Butter for the pan

Instructions:

Mix milk, eggs, and rum. Slowly whisk in flour until smooth. Pour half a ladle into a hot buttered pan. Flip when golden. Enjoy warm!
